    Aerial Dance Photo Sessions with Shinu John

    Join us for an enchanting aerial dance photoshoot featuring the artistic vision of renowned fine art photographer, Shinu John. With his specialization in creating fantasy worlds and empowering women to feel like the best version of themselves, Shinu’s expertise promises to transform aerial dance into an ethereal and captivating experience.

    Under the mentorship and guidance of experienced dancer and choreographer, Kathleen Doherty, participants will have the opportunity to elevate their artistic expression. Kathleen’s personalised coaching will help participants refine their technique and performance quality, ensuring they shine in front of Shinu’s lens. In addition to the photoshoot, participants will gain exclusive access to an online workshop led by Kathleen, focusing on how to prepare for an aerial dance photoshoot. From selecting costumes and makeup to understanding lighting and composition, Kathleen will share invaluable tips to help participants feel confident and radiant on camera.

    Whether you’re an experienced aerialist or new to the art form, this event offers a unique opportunity to collaborate with two industry professionals and create stunning images that embody grace, beauty, and empowerment. Don’t miss this chance to step into a world of fantasy and capture the magic of aerial dance with Shinu John and Kathleen Doherty
